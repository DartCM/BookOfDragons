Description
The boneknapper is a rare dragon that collects and wears bones as armor, making it nearly indestructible. Despite its fearsome appearance, it is surprisingly quiet except for the eerie rattling of its bones. Though elusive and often misunderstood a well trained boneknapper is a loyal and formidable companion.
