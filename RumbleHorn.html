Description
The Rumblehorn is a powerful tracker with an unmatched sense of smell capable of finding anything over vast distances. Its thick armor and ramming horn make it a formidable force, easily breaking through obstacles. This dragon fires explosive molten blasts and is both aggressive and fiercely loyal, making it perfect for search and rescue missions.
